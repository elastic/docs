By following these guidelines for using our trademarks and service marks, you help us protect and strengthen our trademark rights. By using any Elasticsearch trademark, you acknowledge that Elasticsearch is the sole owner of that trademark and you agree not to interfere with Elasticsearch's rights in the trademark anywhere in the world. You also agree that any goodwill derived from using any Elasticsearch trademark inures to the benefit of Elasticsearch.
